Just picking away at a cute lil Minecraft modpack.

Yet another Create-centered modpack, with a focus on extending progression and adding cool vibes, while also sticking to Create's aesthetic instead of throwing in a bunch of the other tech mods that are too futuristic-looking.

Heavily inspired by Create Astral (really cool modpack, but I want a steampunk fantasy adventure, not to go to space) and Raspberry Flavoured (because it's such a cool execution of tightening up Vanilla Minecraft and integrating everything together).
